Personal description

I would describe myself as a highly ambitious, punctual, and persevering individual who always strives to reach their maximum potential or even break their own barriers and exceed their limits. I enjoy communicating and meeting new people, and I would say that working in a team is one of my great strengths because I have the opportunity to socialize, understand my colleagues' ways of thinking, and share ideas. However, most of the time, I prefer to work alone, as I can concentrate better on what needs to be done. I always prioritize other people's time over my own, which is why I am very punctual. I love learning new things about technology and how it influences our lives. Usually, to make my days simpler, especially the busy ones, I choose to organize them into small pieces and know in advance everything that needs to be done. I am a very organized person, and I believe that small details make a difference, at least that is my way of thinking. Because of this, even the smallest thing that is not in its proper place bothers me, and I try to make everything perfect. One thing that represents me is that I can quickly learn absolutely anything that is explained to me, effortlessly. I have a very good visual and auditory memory. Although being human comes with imperfection, I try to make everything perfect around me.
